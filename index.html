About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in the e-Media Research Lab at KU Leuven, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Bart Vanrumste. My research focuses on artificial intelligence for healthcare applications. As part of the FOG-IT project, I develop deep-learning methods to detect and assess freezing of gait in Parkinson’s disease.
