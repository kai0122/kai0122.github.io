About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the e-Media Research Lab at KU Leuven, under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Bart Vanrumste. My research focuses on deep learning, artificial intelligence, and immersive technologies. As part of the FOG‑IT project, I develop machine-learning methods to detect and assess freezing of gait in Parkinson’s disease using wearable sensors.
